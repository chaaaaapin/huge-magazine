# HUGE Magazine — Launch Content Plan

**Created:** February 3, 2026

The first 10 articles that will establish the voice, quality bar, and editorial direction of HUGE Magazine.

---

## Launch Day Content (3 Articles)

### 1. Brand Manifesto: "What Makes an Idea HUGE?"
**Type:** Opinion/Philosophy
**Length:** 2,000 words
**Purpose:** Establish editorial voice, explain what we cover and why

**Outline:**
- Not everything new is breakthrough
- The difference between incremental and transformational
- Our criteria: Scale of impact, timing (why now), accessibility, originality
- What we won't cover (product reviews, funding rounds for funding's sake, hype cycles)
- Invitation to readers: join us in finding what's next

**Visual:** Custom illustration of "small idea vs. HUGE idea" — abstract, bold, memorable

---

### 2. First HUGE Breakthrough Feature: "The Race to Build AI That Does Science"
**Type:** HUGE Breakthrough (signature format)
**Length:** 3,500 words
**Purpose:** Demonstrate our depth and visual ambition

**Angle:** Not another ChatGPT piece. Focus on AI systems designed specifically for scientific discovery — AlphaFold's legacy, FutureHouse, AI2's work, Anthropic's scientific applications.

**Key sections:**
- The hypothesis generation problem
- Case study: From AlphaFold to drug discovery
- Who's building "scientist AI" and how they differ
- What changes when AI can propose experiments
- The meta-question: Will AI accelerate or narrow scientific creativity?

**Visual:** Custom diagrams of the scientific method + AI loop, timeline infographic of AI science milestones

---

### 3. Pillar Page: "The Complete Guide to AI in 2026"
**Type:** Comprehensive guide
**Length:** 5,000+ words
**Purpose:** SEO cornerstone, establish authority, internal linking hub

**Sections:**
1. What AI actually is (and isn't) — clear definitions
2. The major models and who makes them — landscape overview
3. What AI can reliably do today — honest assessment
4. What AI definitely cannot do — cutting through hype
5. The business applications that are real — enterprise focus
6. The safety and alignment debate — fair treatment of concerns
7. Where AI is going — 2-5 year horizon
8. How to think about AI in your work — practical guidance

**Visual:** Interactive model comparison chart, capability matrix, timeline

## Content Quality Checklist

Before any article publishes, it must:

- [ ] Have a clear answer to "why this, why now?"
- [ ] Include at least one expert source or original insight
- [ ] Have custom visual elements (not just stock photos)
- [ ] Link to 3+ internal pages (or be first in cluster)
- [ ] Have a compelling headline that makes a claim
- [ ] Pass the "would I share this?" test
- [ ] Be proofread by someone other than the writer
- [ ] Have proper metadata (title, description, OG image)
